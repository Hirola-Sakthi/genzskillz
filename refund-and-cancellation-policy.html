Refund & Cancellation Policy

Welcome to GenZSkillz, a brand powered by GTR Education for Excellence.
Please read this Refund & Cancellation Policy carefully before subscribing or purchasing any of our online learning programs, courses, or digital content.

  1. No Refund Policy

All purchases, subscriptions, and course enrollments made on GenZSkillz are final and non-refundable.
Once access to a course, subscription, or service is granted, no cancellations, refunds, or transfers will be permitted under any circumstance.

This policy applies to:

  • Online courses and study materials
  • Mock tests and AI learning tools
  • Subscription or membership plans
  • Practice papers, lab access, or digital resources

We encourage users to carefully review course details, demos, and previews (if available) before making a purchase.

  1. Cancellation Policy

Since all our offerings are digitally delivered, there is no option for cancellation once the subscription or course access is activated.
Auto-renewal (if applicable) can be stopped manually before the next billing date through your account settings.

  1. Duplicate or Erroneous Payments

In case a duplicate payment has been made due to a technical error or processing issue, please contact our support team immediately.
After verification, the duplicate amount will be refunded within 7–10 business days.

  1. Technical Access Issues

If you experience any issues accessing your course or subscription, please contact our technical support team.
We will ensure your access is restored promptly — however, this does not qualify for a refund once the service has been activated.

  1. Policy Updates

GenZSkillz reserves the right to modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time without prior notice.
Any updates will be reflected on this page with the revised date.
