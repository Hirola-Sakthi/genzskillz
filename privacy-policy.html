Privacy Policy

Welcome to GenZSkillz, a brand powered by GTR Education for Excellence.
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, mobile application, and services.

  1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, contact number, and login credentials when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, and features used.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase a course or subscription, your payment details are processed securely through our payment partners.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device model, operating system, and IP address to optimize platform performance.

  1. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized learning experiences and course recommendations.
  • Enable communication for updates, offers, and notifications.
  • Improve our platform performance and user interface.
  • Process payments securely and manage your subscriptions.
  • Ensure compliance with legal and safety obligations.

  1. Data Protection and Security

Your data security is our top priority.
We use advanced encryption protocols, secure servers, and regular audits to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  1. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
We may share your data only with:

  • Trusted service providers (such as payment gateways or analytics partners) who assist in delivering our services.
  • Legal authorities when required to comply with applicable laws or regulations.

All third-party partners adhere to strict data protection standards.

  1. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic.
You can control cookie settings through your browser at any time.

  1. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for data usage.
  • Request a copy of your stored data.

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@GenZSkillz.in.

  1. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites.
We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party platforms.

  1. Children’s Privacy

GenZSkillz is intended for students above 13 years of age.
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

  1. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any major changes will be communicated through our website or email notifications.
