Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is GenZSkillz?

GenZSkillz is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help students prepare for CBSE, State Board, JEE, NEET, and programming courses. It provides study materials, mock tests, lab practice, and instant feedback to make learning smarter and more engaging.

Who owns GenZSkillz?

GenZSkillz is a brand under GTR Education for Excellence, a registered company committed to delivering innovative, technology-driven education solutions.

What kind of courses are available on GenZSkillz?

We offer courses for CBSE and State Boards, competitive exams like JEE and NEET, and programming languages such as C, C++, Java, Python, Go, Ruby, Rust, C#, and JavaScript.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes! Upon successful completion of your course or assessment, you’ll receive a GenZSkillz Certificate of Achievement, powered by GTR Education for Excellence.
